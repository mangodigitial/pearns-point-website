Antigua is famed for its 365 beaches — one for every day of the year. But beyond the powder-white sands and turquoise waters lies a vibrant island of English Harbour history, world-class sailing, exceptional dining, and a warm, welcoming culture.

With year-round sunshine, a stable economy, English-speaking population, and excellent international connectivity, Antigua has become one of the most desirable addresses in the Caribbean for discerning investors and families alike.

Island Lifestyle

More Than
a Destination

From world-class sailing regattas to sunset dining on the harbour, Antigua offers a lifestyle that blends Caribbean ease with international sophistication.

World-Class Sailing

Home to Antigua Sailing Week — one of the world's premier regattas — and Nelson's Dockyard, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Exceptional Dining

From beachside grills to sophisticated restaurants, the island's culinary scene blends Caribbean flavours with international cuisine.

Pristine Beaches

From the buzzing sands of Jolly Beach to hidden coves accessible only by boat — an endless variety of coastal paradise.

Yacht & Marina

Antigua's marinas host superyachts from around the world. Charter a vessel for the day or moor your own at one of several luxury harbours.

"Antigua is a place where the pace of life slows, the sunsets never disappoint, and the sea is always just a few steps away."

Beyond the Beach

An Island of
Experiences

Antigua offers far more than sun and sand — it's a vibrant island with a rich history, thriving culture, and endless adventures.

Nelson's Dockyard & English Harbour

A UNESCO World Heritage site and the only continuously working Georgian dockyard in the world. Explore restored naval buildings, waterfront restaurants, and a living piece of maritime history.

UNESCO Heritage Maritime History Restaurants

Sport & Wellness

From championship golf at Cedar Valley to kitesurfing, deep-sea fishing, tennis, and world-class spa retreats — Antigua caters to every level of sporting ambition and relaxation.

Golf Water Sports Spa & Wellness

Nature & Adventure

Hike to Shirley Heights for panoramic sunset views, kayak through mangrove reserves, snorkel coral reefs, or take a helicopter tour of the island's dramatic coastline.
